Compliance Framework

Our commitment to regulatory excellence and compliance standards

1. Regulatory Overview

ePay's operates under comprehensive regulatory oversight and maintains strict compliance with all applicable federal and state laws governing money transmission services. Our compliance framework ensures the highest standards of financial integrity and consumer protection.

2. Federal Regulatory Compliance

Registered with FinCEN as a Money Service Business and fully compliant with Bank Secrecy Act requirements.

  • FinCEN MSB Registration #31000196234567
  • Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) compliance program
  • Customer Identification Program (CIP)
  • Enhanced due diligence procedures

3. Anti-Money Laundering Program

Comprehensive AML program with written policies, designated compliance officer, employee training, and independent testing.

  • Real-time transaction monitoring systems
  • Suspicious Activity Reports (SAR) filing
  • Customer risk assessment and profiling
  • Ongoing compliance training programs

4. State Money Transmitter Licensing

Licensed as a Money Transmitter in all applicable US states with required surety bonds and financial reporting.

  • 50-state licensing compliance
  • Surety bond and net worth requirements
  • Regular examination and reporting
  • Consumer protection standards

5. OFAC Sanctions Compliance

Comprehensive screening against OFAC sanctions lists with real-time monitoring and geographic restrictions.

  • Real-time sanctions screening
  • Daily list updates and monitoring
  • Geographic transaction restrictions
  • Blocked persons identification

6. Data Protection and Privacy

Compliance with GLBA, PCI DSS, and other data protection standards to ensure customer information security.

  • Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act compliance
  • PCI DSS Level 1 certification
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance
  • Advanced encryption and security controls
